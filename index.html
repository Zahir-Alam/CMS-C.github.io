About Us

What make us the best ?

Charsadda Model School and College (CMS&C) is a trusted name in quality education, dedicated to academic excellence and character building. Located in the heart of Charsadda, our institution serves as a second home for students from diverse backgrounds, offering a nurturing environment where learning thrives. Since our establishment, CMS&C has focused on empowering students through a balanced approach to academics, discipline, and co-curricular development. With experienced faculty, modern facilities, and a clear vision, we aim to shape responsible citizens and future leaders. At CMS&C, we believe education is a right—not a privilege—and we are proud to make it accessible to all.
